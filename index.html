Zhiyu Jiang is a Tenured Associate Professor at Northwestern Polytechnical University (NWPU), working on computer vision, remote sensing scene understanding, and intelligent unmanned systems. His recent research emphasizes cross-domain visual learning, few-shot perception, and multimodal semantic understanding for real-world applications.

Research

My current research topics include:

  • Computer vision and remote sensing scene understanding.
  • Few-shot / low-shot learning, cross-domain adaptation, and open-world recognition.
  • Semantic segmentation and change detection for UAV and satellite imagery.
  • Multimodal learning for intelligent unmanned systems and embodied perception.
