Student Supervision

I supervise undergraduate and graduate students working on open-world visual perception, remote sensing image understanding, multimodal learning, autonomous unmanned systems, and visual security. Student projects cover few-shot learning, cross-domain object detection, hyperspectral image classification, UAV perception, visual adversarial attack, and counter-UAV perception.
